The prestigious Cornwallis community, in Cape Coral, is located on the east coast just off the Caloosahatchee River. The Cornwallis Neighborhood Association (CNA) was formed in 1994 with its main purpose dedicated to the improvement and enjoyment of our neighborhood and maintaining all its spectacular beauty and surroundings. CNA is voluntarily comprised of residents, both owners and renters, permanent and seasonal, who are committed to and wish to enhance the beauty, goodwill, and safety of the neighborhood. Activities, gatherings, and general meetings are typically held October thru May. Our general meetings are held at Century 21 Birchwood Realty building located at 4040 Del Prado Blvd @ 7:15 pm. We encourage you to take the time to learn more about becoming a member.
